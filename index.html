Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a session?

You can book directly through the contact form on the website or by reaching out via phone, WhatsApp, or Instagram.

How much advance notice is required for bookings?

At least 7–10 days in advance is recommended to ensure availability, especially during wedding season.

How long does bridal mehendi take?

Usually 3–5 hours depending on design complexity.

Do you offer group bookings?

Yes, group bookings for family functions, baby showers, and festive events are available.
